# Amazon Sales Performance and Analysis
This project focuses on analyzing Amazon sales data to understand business performance, identify trends,and generate actionable insights.The analysis explores product performance, regional sales distribution, customer behavior, and the impact of discount strategies on revenue and profitability.

## Data Cleaning & Preparation
The dataset was cleaned and prepared using Power Query in the Excel:

- Removed duplicate records  
- Handled missing values  
- Standardized date formats  
- Verified numerical fields (sales, profit, quantity)  
- Created additional columns:
  - Discount Buckets 
  - Discount Amount 
- Ensured data consistency and accuracy  

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
The data was analyzed to answer key business questions:

- Sales performance across product categories  
- Regional sales and profit distribution  
- Impact of discount strategies on revenue  
- Customer behavior and purchasing trends  
- Monthly Product Categories Performance

## Key Findings

### Product Performance
- Beauty category generated the highest revenue and profit  
- Sports category showed the lowest performance  
- Sales distribution across categories is relatively balanced  

### Regional Insights
- Middle East is the highest revenue-generating region  
- North America closely follows in performance  
- Europe shows the lowest sales, indicating growth potential  

### Discount Strategy
- Low discounts generate the highest revenue  
- High discounts do not significantly improve sales  
- Balanced pricing strategy performs better than heavy discounting  

### Customer Insights
- Customers respond well to moderate pricing  
- Product ratings influence sales but are not the only factor  
- Repeat demand is strong in categories like Beauty  

### Business Performance
- Total Revenue: 32.86M  
- Total Profit: 1.64M  
- Profit Margin: 5%  
- Average Order Value: 657
